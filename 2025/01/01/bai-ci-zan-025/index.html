考研词汇精选-025

01 atlas

n.地图集

The atlas contains information about all the countries in the world.
地图集包含了世界上所有国家的信息。

02 habitable

adj. 适合居住的；可居住的

The environment of Antarctica is too harsh to be habitable.
南极洲的环境太恶劣了，不适合居住。

It made a large number of islands habitable.
它使许多岛屿适合居住。

03 saturate

v.使充满，使饱和，使湿透

The pouring rain has saturated the soil.
这场瓢泼大雨浸透了土壤。

04 inaugurate

v.开展，投入使用；-为…揭幕；为…举行就职典礼

The airline will inaugurate a new route this summer.
该航空公司将在今年夏天开通一条新航线。

The latter, inaugurated in 2009, has 8,844 photovoltaic panels producing up to 1.14 GWh of electricity annually.
后者于2009年投入使用，拥有8844块光伏板，年发电量高达1.14千兆瓦时。

05 incorporated

adj. 有限责任的；组成公司的；合成一体的；vi.合并；组成公司；vt.合并；组建公司；给予实质

This is an incorporated company.
这是一家有限责任公司。

06 headstrong

adj 任性的；顽固的

He is a headstrong boy who doesn’t take in his parents’ suggestions.
他是一个听不进父母建议的任性男孩。

07 amends

n.赔偿；赔罪

The driver who was responsible for the accident agreed to make full amends.
此次事故的肇事司机同意付全部赔偿。

08 magnitude

n.重要性；规模，大小；巨大；震级；【数】量值

We should learn about the magnitude of the heart and take good care of it.
我们应该意识到心脏的重要性并保护好它。

… no one since has celebrated more eloquently the magnitude of its achievement.
.从那以后，没有人比他更滔滔不绝地庆祝其成就的重要性。

09 obstruct

v.阻碍，妨碍，阻塞，堵塞

The barricade obstructs the road.
路障阻碍了道路。

.., it is obstructed and scattered by the dirt in the water.
·…但它被水中的污垢所阻挡和散射。

10 discrete

adj. 分开的；不连续的

The abacus is made up of discrete units.
算盘是由分开的单元组成的。

Definitions range from discrete descriptions of various types of play ..
定义的范围从对各种游戏类型的离散描述.
