Vocabulary

afro

  • 简易

英/ˈæfrəʊ/美/ˈæfroʊ/n.圆蓬式发型；非洲式发型；adj.（美国）黑人的；圆蓬式的

复数afros

  • 《柯林斯英汉双解大词典》

Afro/ˈæfrəʊ/

1.ADJ Afro hair is very tightly curled and sticks out all around your head. 非洲式的

2.N-COUNT An Afro is an Afro hairstyle. 非洲式卷发

The Afro-Asian nations had approved the basic general principles of nonalignment.亚非国家已经同意了不结盟的基本原则。

Instead of an Afro, his hair was slicked and combed to one side, and he wore mirrored aviator sunglasses.他的头发不是爆炸头，而是光溜溜地梳向一边，他还戴了一副反光飞行员墨镜。

ginger

  • 简易

英/ˈdʒɪndʒə(r)/美/ˈdʒɪndʒər/n.生姜；姜（产于东南亚，外表似竹）；姜黄色；长着姜黄色头发的人；姜味汽水（ginger ale 的简称）；精神，活力；v.用姜给……调味；激励，使有生气（ginger sb./sth. up）；adj.
（主要指毛发或动物毛皮）姜黄色的；姜味的；【名】 （Ginger）（法）然热，（英）金杰（人名）

复数gingers第三人称单数gingers现在分词gingering过去式gingered过去分词gingered

  • 《柯林斯英汉双解大词典》

ginger/ˈdʒɪndʒə/|CET6+ TEM4
1.N-UNCOUNT Ginger is the root of a plant that is used to flavour food. It has a sweet, spicy flavour and is often sold in powdered form. 姜

2.COLOR Ginger is used to describe things that are orangey brown in colour. 姜黄色的

• She was a mature lady with dyed ginger hair.
她是个成年女子，头发染成了姜黄色。

blonde

  • 简易

英/blɒnd/美/blɑːnd/adj.金黄色的，浅棕色的；金发的；浅色的；n.长着金发的人

高中 | CET4 | CET6 | IELTS
复数blondes比较级blonder最高级blondest

  • 《柯林斯英汉双解大词典》

blonde/blɒnd/|CET6+ TEM4
blonder blondest blondes
1.COLOR A woman who has blonde hair has pale-coloured hair. Blonde hair can be very light brown or light yellow. The form blond is used when describing men. 浅色的; (头发) 金黄色的

• …a little girl with blonde hair.
…一头金发的小女孩。

2.ADJ Someone who is blonde has blonde hair. 金发的

• He was blonder than his brother.
他的头发比哥哥的还黄。

3.N-COUNT A blonde is a woman who has blonde hair. 金发女子

• …a stunning blonde in her early thirties.
…一位美貌惊人的三十出头的金发女郎。
