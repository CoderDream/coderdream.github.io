Task 4

Clothes 2 - Write the words

Write the correct word from the other Clothes 2 tasks.

  • Question
  1. You can’t put on [] after you’ve put on [] .
  2. Both men and women wear shoes and sandals, but usually only women wear [] .
  3. In hot weather, [] are cooler than trousers and [] are cooler than shoes.
  4. In many companies, men must wear a [] to work.
  5. A [] goes on the head and a [] goes around the neck.
  6. In a traditional Indian wedding, most women wear a [] .
  • Answer
  1. You can’t put on [socks] after you’ve put on [shoes|boots].
  2. Both men and women wear shoes and sandals, but usually only women wear [high heels] .
  3. In hot weather, [shorts] are cooler than trousers and [sandals] are cooler than shoes.
  4. In many companies, men must wear a [suit|tie] to work.
  5. A [headscarf] goes on the head and a [tie] goes around the neck.
  6. In a traditional Indian wedding, most women wear a [sari].

sari/ˈsɑːrɪ/
N-COUNT A sari is a piece of clothing worn especially by Indian women. It consists of a long piece of thin material that is wrapped around the body. (印度妇女穿的)莎丽
