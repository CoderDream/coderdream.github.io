Task 4-任务四

Weather - Write the words

Write the words to complete the sentences.

  • Question
  1. If the temperature is low, it is ().
  2. The () is big and yellow. It rises in the morning and sets at night.
  3. When it is () , it is quite hot, but not too hot.
  4. A () is a white or grey shape in the sky.
  5. () is water that falls from the sky.
  6. () is like a cloud, but is near the ground.
  7. After it rains, the ground is () .
  8. In a () there is thunder, lightning and often heavy rain.
  9. The () is moving air – it blows our hair and the leaves on plants.
  10. When it has not rained recently, the ground is () .
  • Answer
  1. If the temperature is low, it is (cold).
  2. The (sun) is big and yellow. It rises in the morning and sets at night.
  3. When it is (warm) , it is quite hot, but not too hot.
  4. A (cloud) is a white or grey shape in the sky.
  5. (Rain) is water that falls from the sky.
  6. (Fog) is like a cloud, but is near the ground.
  7. After it rains, the ground is (wet) .
  8. In a (storm) there is thunder, lightning and often heavy rain.
  9. The (wind) is moving air – it blows our hair and the leaves on plants.
  10. When it has not rained recently, the ground is (dry) .

Task 5-任务五

Weather - Nouns and adjectives

Write the noun forms of the adjectives.

  • Question
  1. cloudy → __
  2. foggy → __
  3. rainy → __
  4. snowy → __
  5. stormy → __
  6. sunny → __
  7. windy → __
  • Answer
  1. cloudy → cloud
  2. foggy → fog
  3. rainy → rain
  4. snowy → snow
  5. stormy → storm
  6. sunny → sun
  7. windy → wind
