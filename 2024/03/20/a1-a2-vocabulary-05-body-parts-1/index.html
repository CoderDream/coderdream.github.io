Complete the sentences.

  • Question

  1. We use our [ ] to hear.

  2. Most people have two [ ] . Our hands are at the end.

  3. Most people have five [ ] on each hand.

  4. After our mouth, our food goes down our [ ] . Sometimes it hurts when we are ill.

  5. Most people have two [ ] . We use them to walk.

  6. We have a [ ] in the middle of our face. We use it to smell.

  7. We use our [ ] to see.

  10. Our [ ] has our hair, mouth, nose, eyes and ears on it.

  • Answer
  1. We use our [ears] to hear.
  2. Most people have two [arms] . Our hands are at the end.
  3. Most people have five [fingers] on each hand.
  4. After our mouth, our food goes down our [throat] . Sometimes it hurts when we are ill.
  5. Most people have two [legs] . We use them to walk.
  6. We have a [nose] in the middle of our face. We use it to smell.
  7. We use our [eyes] to see.
  8. We wear a shoe on each [foot] .
  9. We use our [mouth] to talk, smile and eat.
  10. Our [head] has our hair, mouth, nose, eyes and ears on it.
