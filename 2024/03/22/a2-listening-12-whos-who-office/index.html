【A2-Listening】12 Who’s who in the office-办公室里有谁

Listen to two people talking about the people in their office to practise and improve your listening skills.

Do the preparation task first. Then listen to the audio and do the exercises.

Transcript
English


I want to explain a few things about your essay.


First of all, the deadline. The deadline for this essay is October the 18th. Not the 19th, not the 28th, not two days later because your dog was ill or your computer broke – the 18th. If it’s late, I won’t mark it. I won’t even read it – you’ll fail the assignment! So, please hand it in on time. You can even hand it in early, if you like!


You can email me the essays at j.hartshorn@lmu.ac.uk. That’s H-A-R-T-S-H-O-R-N. I’ll reply to say I’ve got it. If I don’t reply within a day, it might mean I didn’t get it, so please email me again to make sure. You can also bring a paper copy of the essay to my office, but let’s be kind to the trees, OK? Email is better for the trees and for me.


Don’t forget that you must reference every idea or quote you use that isn’t your own idea. And the last page of your essay should be a list of all the books you used, in alphabetical order, not in the order you used them!


And lastly, make it easy for me to read! That means use a clear font. Arial is best, but Times New Roman is fine too. Not Comic Sans please! Size 12 font for the essay, and size 14 for the titles and subheadings. And use page numbers. Any questions?




English-Chinese


Jani: Welcome to the department, Yuki. Let me show you around and tell you about your new colleagues. I’ll introduce you to them all later.
Jani：欢迎来到这个部门，Yuki。让我带您参观一下并介绍一下您的新同事。稍后我将向您介绍他们。


Yuki: Great. Thanks. So, who are those people over there?
由纪：太好了。谢谢。那么，那边的人是谁？


Jani: That’s the order management team. Luciana deals with the new orders. She’s the one with short, dark hair.
Jani：那是订单管理团队。卢西亚娜处理新订单。她是那个有着黑色短发的人。


Yuki: In the purple dress?
Yuki：穿着紫色裙子？


Jani: Yeah, that’s right. You’ll probably work closely with her while you are learning about our ordering process.
贾尼：是的，没错。在了解我们的订购流程时，您可能会与她密切合作。


Yuki: Got it, thanks. And who’s that over there? The guy who’s on the phone?
Yuki：明白了，谢谢。那边是谁？打电话的那个人？


Jani: Oh, you mean the one by the window? In the green shirt? Ah, that’s Ian. He’s the marketing director. And that’s Maria beside him. She’s responsible for the internal IT systems.
贾尼：哦，你是说靠窗的那个吗？穿绿衬衫？啊，那是伊恩。他是营销总监。他旁边就是玛丽亚。她负责内部 IT 系统。


Yuki: OK, I’ll try to remember all of this. I should probably be taking notes!
Yuki：好吧，我会尽力记住这一切。我可能应该记笔记！


Jani: Don’t worry about it. For now it’s just good to put some faces to names.
贾尼：别担心。现在最好在名字上添加一些面孔。


Yuki: OK, great – and who do I talk to about setting up my mobile phone with email access? Is that also Maria?
Yuki：好的，太好了——我应该和谁讨论如何设置我的手机以访问电子邮件？那也是玛丽亚吗？


Jani: No, you need to talk to Sebastian who works in communications. He can help you. That’s him over there, next to the printer.
贾尼：不，你需要和从事传播工作的塞巴斯蒂安谈谈。他可以帮助你。那是他，就在打印机旁边。


Yuki: Thanks. I’ll introduce myself to him later.
由希：谢谢。稍后我会向他介绍我自己。
