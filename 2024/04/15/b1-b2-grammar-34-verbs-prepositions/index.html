Grammar explanation语法解释

When a verb is part of a longer sentence, it is often followed by a specific preposition. 当动词是较长句子的一部分时，它后面通常会跟一个特定的介词。

I agree with Mike.我同意迈克的观点。
She listens to the radio a lot.她经常听广播。
He thanked me for the flowers.他感谢我送花。

There are no grammatical rules to help you know which preposition is used with which verb, so it’s a good idea to try to learn them together. To help you do this, write new vocabulary in your notebook in a sentence or phrase. Here are some common verbs for each preposition.没有语法规则可以帮助您了解哪个介词与哪个动词一起使用，因此最好尝试一起学习它们。为了帮助您做到这一点，请在笔记本上用句子或短语写下新词汇。以下是每个介词的一些常见动词。

Verbs with for带for的动词

They’re waiting for a bus.他们正在等公共汽车。
He apologised for being late. 他为迟到而道歉。
I applied for the job but I didn’t get it.我申请了这份工作，但没有得到。
How do you ask for a coffee in Polish?波兰语 怎样要一杯咖啡？
I can’t go out tonight because I have to prepare for my interview tomorrow.我今晚不能出去，因为我要准备明天的面试。

Verbs with from带 from 的动词

This spray should protect you from mosquitoes.这种喷雾可以保护您免受蚊子侵害。
Has he recovered from the accident yet?他从事故中恢复了吗？
She won an award because she saved someone from drowning.她因为救了一个溺水的人而获奖。
I suffer from allergies.我患有过敏症。

Verbs with in带 in 的动词

She doesn’t believe in coincidences.她不相信巧合。
Our company specialises in computer software.我们公司专业从事计算机软件。
You have to work hard if you want to succeed in life.如果你想在生活中取得成功，你就必须努力工作。

Verbs with of带 of 的动词

I don’t approve of hunting animals for their fur.我不赞成为了毛皮而狩猎动物。
Our dog died of old age.我们的狗因年老而死了。
This shampoo smells of bananas.这款洗发水有香蕉味。

Verbs with on带 on 的动词

Their decision will depend on the test results.他们的决定将取决于测试结果。
The film is based on the novel by Boris Pasternak.该片根据鲍里斯·帕斯捷尔纳克的小说改编。
If you make so much noise, I can’t concentrate on my work.如果你发出这么大的噪音，我就无法专心工作。
Come on! We’re relying on you!快点！我们就靠你了！
We don’t agree on anything but we’re still good friends.我们在任何事情上都没有达成共识，但我们仍然是好朋友。

Verbs with to带to的动词

What kind of music do you like listening to?你喜欢听什么类型的音乐？
Can I introduce you to my grandfather?我可以把你介绍给我的祖父吗？
Please refer to the notes at the end for more information.请参阅最后的注释以获取更多信息。
Nobody responded to my complaint.没有人回应我的投诉。
She apologised to me the next day.第二天她就向我道歉了。

Verbs with with动词 with

I agree with everything you’ve said.我同意你所说的一切。
My assistant will provide you with more information if you need it.如果您需要，我的助理将为您提供更多信息。
We’re finding it difficult to deal with the stress.我们发现很难应对压力。
