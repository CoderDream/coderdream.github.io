【A1-A2-Grammar】03 Articles: ‘a’, ‘an’, ‘the’-冠词

Do you know how to use a, an and the? Test what you know with interactive exercises and read the explanation to help you.你知道如何使用 a、an 和 the 吗？通过互动练习测试您所掌握的知识，并阅读说明来帮助您。

Look at these examples to see how articles are used.查看这些示例以了解如何使用冠词。

She’s a doctor.她是一名医生。
I need an umbrella.我需要一把雨伞。
Have you heard the news?你听说（这件新闻）了吗？
I don’t like spiders.我不喜欢蜘蛛。

Grammar test 1

Articles 1: 1

Complete the sentences with ‘a’, ‘an’ or ‘the’, or ‘-‘ if no article is needed.

  • Question
  1. I like listening to (1) music. It helps me relax.
  2. (2) money he gave me wasn’t enough.
  3. He doesn’t like (3) dogs. One bit him when he was a child.
  4. Could you please pass me (4) salt?
  5. Do you want (5) apple? I’ve got two in my bag.
  6. I can take (6) children to school today.
  7. This is my uncle Phil. He’s (7) teacher and he lives in London.
  8. Is there (8) university where you live?
  • Answer
  1. I like listening to (-) music. It helps me relax.
  2. (The) money he gave me wasn’t enough.
  3. He doesn’t like (-) dogs. One bit him when he was a child.
  4. Could you please pass me (the) salt?
  5. Do you want (an) apple? I’ve got two in my bag.
  6. I can take (the) children to school today.
  7. This is my uncle Phil. He’s (a) teacher and he lives in London.
  8. Is there (a) university where you live?

Grammar explanation语法解释

Here are some of the most important things to know about using articles.以下是有关使用文章需要了解的一些最重要的事项。

Jobs工作

When we say what people’s jobs are, we usually use a/an.当我们说人们的工作是什么时，我们通常使用 a/an。

He’s an architect.他是一名建筑师。
She’s a scientist.她是一位科学家。
My grandmother was a teacher.我的祖母是一名教师。

Singular nouns单数名词

Singular, countable nouns always have an article – a/an or the (or another determiner – my, your, this, that, etc.).单数、可数名词总是有冠词 – a/an 或 the（或另一个限定词 – my、your、this、that 等）。

We use a/an – the indefinite article – when we talk about something for the first time, or something that is part of a group or type.当我们第一次谈论某件事或某个群体或类型的一部分时，我们使用 a/an（不定冠词）。

I saw a good film yesterday.昨天我看了一部好电影。
Do you want a drink?你想喝一杯吗？

We use a when the word that follows it begins with a consonant sound. We use an when it’s followed by a vowel sound. This makes pronunciation easier.当后面的单词以辅音开头时，我们使用 a。当后面跟着元音时我们使用 an。这使得发音更容易。

She has a university degree.她拥有大学学位。
It took me an hour to get home.我花了一个小时才到家。

We use the – the definite article – when the listener already knows which thing we are talking about because it was mentioned before or because there’s only one of them.当听众已经知道我们在谈论哪件事时，我们使用定冠词，因为它之前已经提到过，或者因为只有其中一个。

I’m going to take the dog for a walk.我要带狗去散步。
Have you seen the car key?你看到车钥匙了吗？
They go to the school next to the bridge.他们去了桥旁边的学校。

Things in general一般事物

When we talk about things in general, we normally use a plural or uncountable noun with no article.当我们谈论一般事物时，我们通常使用复数或不可数名词，不带冠词。

Birds eat worms.鸟吃虫子。
Water freezes at 0°C.水在 0°C 时结冰。
Children need a lot of sleep.孩子们需要大量的睡眠。

Particular groups of things特定的事物组

When we talk about a particular group of things, we use the.当我们谈论一组特定的事物时，我们使用。

We went to the zoo and saw the kangaroos. (These are the particular kangaroos in that zoo – not kangaroos in general.)我们去了动物园，看到了袋鼠。 （这些是动物园里的特殊袋鼠，而不是一般的袋鼠。）

Grammar test 2

Articles 1: 2

Complete the sentences with ‘a’, ‘an’ or ‘the’, or ‘-‘ if no article is needed.

  • Question
  1. Do you want (1) sandwich? I’ve got cheese and bread in the shopping bag.
  2. She wants to be (2) ambulance driver when she finishes school.
  3. Did you see (3) moon last night?
  4. I really hate (4) mosquitos. They always bite me.
  5. If you need to contact me over the weekend, please send me (5) email.
  6. I’m a fun-loving person. I love (6) parties and dancing!
  7. I’ll be there in (7) hour.
  8. (8) teachers at my son’s school are great.
  • Answer
  1. Do you want (a) sandwich? I’ve got cheese and bread in the shopping bag.
  2. She wants to be (an) ambulance driver when she finishes school.
  3. Did you see (the) moon last night?
  4. I really hate (-) mosquitos. They always bite me.
  5. If you need to contact me over the weekend, please send me (an) email.
  6. I’m a fun-loving person. I love (-) parties and dancing!
  7. I’ll be there in (an) hour.
  8. (The) teachers at my son’s school are great.
