Grammar explanation语法解释

We use I/you/we/they + have got or he/she/it + has got to talk about things that we have.我们用“我/你/我们/他们+已经拥有”或“他/她/它+必须”来谈论我们拥有的事情。

In many situations, have and have got mean the same thing. Have got is a little less formal than have. We often use have got more in speaking and have more in writing.在许多情况下，have 和 have got 表示相同的意思。 Have got 比have 稍微不那么正式。我们经常在口语中使用have got more，在写作中使用have more。

They have got a big garden. = They have a big garden.他们有一个大花园。 等于他们有一个大花园。
She has got a good job. = She has a good job.她找到了一份好工作。 等于她有一份好工作。

We can use have/has got to talk about appearance, family and friends or possessions. We often contract the verb, especially when we’re speaking.我们可以使用 have/has had 来谈论外表、家人和朋友或财产。我们经常缩写动词，尤其是在说话时。

I have got brown hair. = I‘ve got* brown hair.我有一头棕色的头发。 = 我有一头棕色的头发。
My sister has got long hair. = My sister**’s got** long hair.我姐姐有长头发。 = 我姐姐的头发很长。
He has not got many friends. = He hasn’t got many friends.他没有很多朋友。 = 他没有很多朋友。
They have got a new car. = They**’ve got** a new car.他们有一辆新车。 = 他们有一辆新车。*

We can also use have/has got for timetabled events, illnesses or abstract things.我们还可以使用 has/has got 来表示已安排的事件、疾病或抽象事物。

They‘ve got* basketball practice this evening.他们今晚有篮球训练。
He**’s got** a terrible cold. 他得了重感冒。
I**’ve got** a good idea!我有个好主意！*

Negative否定

For negatives, use not after have/has. We can contract negatives.对于否定词，不要在 has/has 之后使用 not 。我们可以收缩否定。

We have not got any pets. = We have*n’t got any pets.我们没有养宠物。 = 我们没有任何宠物。
She has not got a brother. = She hasn’t got a brother.她没有兄弟。 = 她没有兄弟。*

Questions疑问句

To make questions, use have/has + subject + got.要提出问题，请使用 has/has + 主语 + got。

Have you got a big house?你有大房子吗？
How many rooms has it got?它有多少个房间？

Short answers简短的答案

For yes/no questions, we can use short answers.对于是/否问题，我们可以使用简短的答案。

*Have you got a new job? **No, I haven’t.**你找到新工作了吗？不，我没有。
Has she got a meeting this morning? **Yes, she has.*她今天早上有会议吗？是的，她有。

Yes, I/you/we/they have.是的，我/你/我们/他们有。 No, I/you/we/they haven’t.不，我/你/我们/他们没有。
Yes, he/she/it has.是的，他/她/它有。 No, he/she/it hasn’t.不，他/她/它没有。

Have/has got is only used in the present tense. For the past, use had without got.Have/has got 仅用于现在时。对于过去，使用 had 而不使用 got。

I had got a red bike when I was young.我年轻时有一辆红色自行车。
I had a red bike when I was young.我年轻时有一辆红色自行车。

Grammar test 2-语法测试2

Countable and uncountable nouns 1: 1

Complete the sentences with one word.

  • Question
  1. How (1) people are coming?
  2. We’ve got (2) chicken but there isn’t (3) rice.
  3. How (4) water do you think we need?
  4. There’s (5) cat in the garden.
  5. I’ve bought (6) new shoes.
  6. Do you have (7) advice for me?
  7. We have a (8) of chairs but we don’t have (9) tables.
  8. There isn’t (10) toothpaste.

  • Answer
  1. How (many) people are coming?
  2. We’ve got (some) chicken but there isn’t (any|much) rice.
  3. How (much) water do you think we need?
  4. There’s (a) cat in the garden.
  5. I’ve bought (some) new shoes.
  6. Do you have (any|some) advice for me?
  7. We have a (lot) of chairs but we don’t have (any|many) tables.
  8. There isn’t (any|much) toothpaste.
