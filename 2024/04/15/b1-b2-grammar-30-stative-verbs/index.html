Grammar explanation语法解释

Stative verbs describe a state rather than an action. They aren’t usually used in the present continuous form.静态动词描述状态而不是动作。它们通常不以现在进行时形式使用。

I don’t know the answer. I’m not knowing the answer.我不知道答案。我不知道答案。
She really likes you. She’s really liking you.她真的很喜欢你。她真的很喜欢你。
He seems happy at the moment. He’s seeming happy at the moment.他此刻看起来很高兴。他此刻看起来很高兴。

Stative verbs often relate to:静态动词通常涉及：

  • thoughts and opinions: agree, believe, doubt, guess, imagine, know, mean, recognise, remember, suspect, think, understand想法和意见：同意、相信、怀疑、猜测、想象、知道、意味着、认识、记住、怀疑、思考、理解
  • feelings and emotions: dislike, hate, like, love, prefer, want, wish感觉和情绪：不喜欢、讨厌、喜欢、爱、喜欢、想要、希望
  • senses and perceptions: appear, be, feel, hear, look, see, seem, smell, taste感官和知觉：出现、存在、感觉、听到、看、看到、似乎、闻到、尝到
  • possession and measurement: belong, have, measure, own, possess, *weigh.*占有和测量：属于、拥有、测量、拥有、占有、称重。

Verbs that are sometimes stative有时是静态的动词

A number of verbs can refer to states or actions, depending on the context.许多动词可以指状态或动作，具体取决于上下文。

I think it’s a good idea.我认为这是个好主意。
Wait a moment! I’m thinking.稍等！我在想。

The first sentence expresses an opinion. It is a mental state, so we use present simple. In the second example the speaker is actively processing thoughts about something. It is an action in progress, so we use present continuous.第一句话表达了一个观点。这是一种心理状态，所以我们用一般现在时。在第二个例子中，说话者正在积极地处理对某事的想法。这是一个正在进行的动作，所以我们用现在进行时。

Some other examples are:其他一些例子是：

have有

I have an old car. (state – possession)我有一辆旧车。 （状态——占有）
I’m having a quick break.我要快速休息一下。(action – having a break is an activity)（行动——休息是一项活动）

see看

Do you see any problems with that? (state – opinion)您认为这有什么问题吗？ （状态-意见）
We’re seeing Tadanari tomorrow afternoon.明天下午我们要见忠成。(action – we’re meeting him)（行动——我们要见他了）

be是

He’s so interesting! (state – his permanent quality)他太有趣了！ （状态——他永恒的品质）
He’s being very unhelpful.他非常无助。(action – he is temporarily behaving this way)（行动——他暂时有这样的行为）

taste品尝

This coffee tastes delicious. (state – our perception of the coffee)这咖啡味道鲜美。 （状态——我们对咖啡的看法）
Look! The chef is tasting the soup看！厨师正在品尝汤. (action – tasting the soup is an activity)。 （行动——品尝汤是一项活动）

Other verbs like this include: agree, appear, doubt, feel, guess, hear, imagine, look, measure, remember, smell, weigh, *wish.*其他类似的动词包括：同意、出现、怀疑、感觉、猜测、听到、想象、看、测量、记住、闻、称重、希望。
