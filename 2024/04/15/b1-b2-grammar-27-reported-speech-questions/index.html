【B1-B2-Grammar】27 Reported speech questions-报告发言 提问

Do you know how to report a question that somebody asked? Test what you know with interactive exercises and read the explanation to help you. 您知道如何报告某人提出的问题吗？ 通过互动练习测试您所掌握的知识，并阅读说明来帮助您。

Look at these examples to see how we can tell someone what another person asked.查看这些示例，了解我们如何告诉某人另一个人问的问题。

direct speech: ‘Do you work from home?’ he said. 直接引语：“你在家工作吗？”他说。
indirect speech: He asked me if I worked from home. 间接引语：他问我是否在家工作。

direct speech: ‘Who did you see?’ she asked. 直接引语：“你看到谁了？”她问。
indirect speech: She asked me who I’d seen. 间接引语：她问我看到了谁。

direct speech: ‘Could you write that down for me?’ she asked. 直接引语：“你能为我写下来吗？”她问。
indirect speech: She asked me to write it down. 间接引语：她让我写下来。

Grammar explanation语法解释

A reported question is when we tell someone what another person asked. To do this, we can use direct speech or indirect speech.报告问题是当我们告诉某人另一个人问的问题时。为此，我们可以使用直接引语或间接引语。

direct speech: ‘Do you like working in sales?’ he asked. 直接引语：“你喜欢销售工作吗？”他问。
indirect speech: He asked me if I liked working in sales. 间接引语：他问我是否喜欢从事销售工作。

In indirect speech, we change the question structure (e.g. Do you like) to a statement structure (e.g. I like).在间接引语中，我们将问题结构（例如，你喜欢吗）更改为陈述结构（例如，我喜欢）。

We also often make changes to the tenses and other words in the same way as for reported statements (e.g. have done → had done, today → that day). You can learn about these changes on the Reported speech 1 – statements page.我们还经常以与报道性陈述相同的方式改变时态和其他词语（例如，已经做了→已经做了，今天→那天）。您可以在报告演讲 1 – 声明页面了解这些变化。

Yes/no questions是/否问题

In yes/no questions, we use if or whether to report the question. If is more common.在是/否问题中，我们使用是否或是否报告问题。如果是比较常见的。

‘Are you going to the Helsinki conference?’ “你要去赫尔辛基会议吗？”

  • He asked me if I was going to the Helsinki conference. 他问我是否要去参加赫尔辛基会议。

‘Have you finished the project yet?’ “你完成这个项目了吗？”

  • She asked us whether we’d finished the project yet. 她问我们这个项目是否完成了。

Questions with a question word-带有疑问词的疑问句

In what, where, why, who, when or how questions, we use the question word to report the question.在什么、哪里、为什么、谁、何时或如何的问题中，我们使用疑问词来报告问题。

‘What time does the train leave?’ “火车什么时候离开？”

  • He asked me what time the train left. 他问我火车几点出发。

‘Where did he go?’ “他去哪了？”

  • She asked where he went. 她问他去哪儿了。

Reporting verbs-报告动词

The most common reporting verb for questions is ask, but we can also use verbs like enquire, want to know or wonder.最常见的表达问题的动词是“询问”，但我们也可以使用“询问”、“想知道”或“想知道”等动词。

‘Did you bring your passports?’ “你带护照了吗？”

  • She wanted to know if they’d brought their passports. 她想知道他们是否带了护照。

‘When could you get this done by?’ “你什么时候能完成这件事？”

  • He wondered when we could get it done by. 他想知道我们什么时候能完成这件事。

Offers, requests and suggestions-报价、请求和建议

If the question is making an offer, request or suggestion, we can use a specific verb pattern instead, for example offer + infinitive, ask + infinitive or suggest + ing.如果问题是提出要约、请求或建议，我们可以使用特定的动词模式，例如 Offer + 不定式、ask + 不定式或 suggest + ing。

‘Would you like me to help you?’ “你想要我帮助你吗？”

  • He offered to help me. 他主动提出要帮助我。

‘Can you hold this for me, please?‘ “你能帮我拿着这个吗？”

  • She asked me to hold it. 她让我拿着它。

‘Why don’t we check with Joel?’ “我们为什么不问问乔尔呢？”

  • She suggested checking with Joel. 她建议与乔尔核实。
