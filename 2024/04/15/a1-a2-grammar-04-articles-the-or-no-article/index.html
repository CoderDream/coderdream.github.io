Look at these examples to see when the is and isn’t used.查看这些示例以了解何时使用和不使用。

I’m going to bed.我去睡觉了。
I walk to work.我步行去上班。
My children are going to start school.我的孩子们要开始上学了。
I visited the school yesterday.我昨天参观了学校。

Grammar explanation语法解释

Here are some ways we use articles in common phrases and place names.以下是我们在常用短语和地名中使用冠词的一些方法。

Common phrases常用短语

We don’t usually use an article in expressions with bed, work and home.我们通常不会在表示 bed、work 和 home 的表达中使用冠词。

go to bed / be in bed上床睡觉/上床睡觉
go to work / be at work / start work / finish work去上班/正在工作/开始工作/完成工作
go home / be at home / get home / stay at home回家/待在家里/回家/呆在家里

We also don’t normally use an article in expressions with school, university, prison and hospital.我们通常也不在学校、大学、监狱和医院的表达中使用冠词。

start school / go to school / be at school开始上学/去学校/在学校
go to university / be at university上大学/在大学
be sent to prison / go to prison / be in prison被送进监狱/入狱/入狱
go to hospital / be in hospital去医院/住院

But we usually use the if someone is just visiting the place, and not there as a student/prisoner/patient, etc.但如果有人只是访问该地方，而不是作为学生/囚犯/病人等，我们通常会使用这个词。

My son has started school now. I went to the school to meet his teacher.我儿子现在已经开始上学了。我去学校见他的老师。
I went to the prison a lot when I was a social worker.当我担任社会工作者时，我经常去监狱。
I’m at the hospital. My sister has just had a baby.我在医院。我姐姐刚刚生完孩子。

Place names地名

We don’t normally use an article for continents, most countries, cities, towns, lakes, mountains or universities. So, we say:我们通常不会用冠词来表示各大洲、大多数国家、城市、城镇、湖泊、山脉或大学。所以，我们说：

Africa, Asia, Europe非洲、亚洲、欧洲
India, Ghana, Peru, Denmark印度、加纳、秘鲁、丹麦
Addis Ababa, Hanoi, New York, Moscow亚的斯亚贝巴、河内、纽约、莫斯科
Lake Victoria, Lake Superior, Lake Tanganyika维多利亚湖、苏必利尔湖、坦噶尼喀湖
Mount Everest, Mount Kilimanjaro, Mount Elbrus珠穆朗玛峰、乞力马扎罗山、厄尔布鲁士山
Cardiff University, Harvard University, Manchester University卡迪夫大学、哈佛大学、曼彻斯特大学

Some countries are different. Country names with United have the. There are other countries which are exceptions too. So, we say:有些国家有所不同。国家名称中带有 United 的。还有其他国家也属于例外。所以，我们说：

the United Arab Emirates, the United Kingdom, the United States of America阿拉伯联合酋长国、英国、美利坚合众国
the Bahamas, the Gambia巴哈马、冈比亚

Seas and oceans, mountain ranges and rivers have the:海洋、山脉和河流有the：

the Atlantic, the Pacific, the Mediterranean大西洋、太平洋、地中海
the Andes, the Himalayas, the Alps安第斯山脉、喜马拉雅山脉、阿尔卑斯山
the Nile, the Amazon, the Yangtze尼罗河、亚马逊河、长江

Universities with of in the title also have the:标题中带有 的大学还有：

the University of Cape Town, the University of Delhi, the University of Tokyo开普敦大学、德里大学、东京大学
