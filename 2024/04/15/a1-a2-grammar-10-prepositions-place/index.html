Grammar explanation语法解释

We can use the prepositions in, on and at to say where things are. They go before nouns. 我们可以使用介词 in、on 和 at 来表示事物所在的位置。它们位于名词之前。

I am in the kitchen. 我在厨房。
My dog likes sleeping on the sofa. 我的狗喜欢睡在沙发上。
The children eat lunch at school. 孩子们在学校吃午餐。

in在

We use in to talk about a place that is inside a bigger space, such as a box, a house, a city or a country. 我们用 in 来谈论更大空间内的地方，例如盒子、房子、城市或国家。

The clothes are in the wardrobe. 衣服在衣柜里。
The children are playing in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩耍。
There’s a bookshop in the shopping centre. 购物中心有一家书店。
My grandmother was born in Sweden. 我的祖母出生在瑞典。

We also use in with other physical locations such as: 我们使用 in 与其他物理位置一起使用，例如：

in the world 在世界上
in water / the sea / a river / a lake / a pool 在水/海/河/湖/池
in the mountains / the countryside / a valley / the forest/ 在山脉/乡村/山谷/森林
in a car / a taxi 在汽车/出租车里

on在

We use on to talk about location on a surface. 我们用 on 来谈论表面上的位置。

The books are on the desk. 书在桌子上。
We live on the fifth floor. 我们住在五楼。
There are pictures on the wall. 墙上有照片。
She likes to sit on the floor. 她喜欢坐在地板上。

We also use on for some types of public transport.我们还使用 on 来乘坐某些类型的公共交通工具。

He’s on the bus now. 他现在在公共汽车上。
You can’t make phone calls on a plane. 飞机上不能打电话。
They go to school on the train. 他们坐火车去学校。

We also use on for lines (including rivers, borders, streets, etc.) and islands.我们还用 on 来表示线条（包括河流、边界、街道等）和岛屿。

London is on the River Thames. 伦敦位于泰晤士河畔。
The Pyrenees are on the border of Spain and France. 比利牛斯山脉位于西班牙和法国的边界。
There’s a market on James Street. 詹姆斯街有一个市场。
I’d love to live on the Isle of Wight. 我很想住在怀特岛。

at 在

We use at in many common phrases, especially when we are talking about a place for a specific activity.我们在许多常用短语中使用 at，尤其是当我们谈论进行特定活动的地点时。

I’m at work. 我在工作。
She’s working at home today. 她今天在家工作。
The children are at school. 孩子们正在上学。
See you at the train station! 火车站见！
They’re at the supermarket. 他们在超市。
I met him at a party. 我是在一次聚会上认识他的。

We also use at for addresses or exact positions.我们还使用 at 来表示地址或确切位置。

I live at 15 Craig Street. 我住在克雷格街 15 号。
She’s sitting at a desk. 她坐在办公桌前。
He’s waiting at the entrance. 他在入口处等着。
Please sit at the back of the room. 请坐在房间的后面。
