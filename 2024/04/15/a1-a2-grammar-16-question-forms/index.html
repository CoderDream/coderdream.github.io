Look at these examples to see how questions are made.查看这些示例，了解如何提出问题。

Grammar explanation语法解释

To make questions, we often put the verb before the subject. This is called inversion.为了提出疑问，我们经常将动词放在主语之前。这称为反转。

Affirmative肯定 Question问题
I am late.我迟到了。 Am I late?我迟了吗？
I can help.我可以搭把手。 Can I help?我是否能帮助？
She is sleeping.她在睡觉。 Is she sleeping?她在睡觉吗？
We have met before.我们以前见过面。 Have we met before?我们以前见过么？

If there is a question word (why, what, where, how, etc.), it goes before the verb.如果有疑问词（为什么、什么、哪里、如何等），则放在动词之前。

Question问题 Question with question word带有疑问词的问题
Are you late?你迟到了吗？ Why are you late?你为什么迟到？
Was she there?她在吗？ When was she there?她什么时候在那儿的？
Can I help?我是否能帮助？ How can I help?我能提供什么帮助吗？
Have we met before?我们以前见过么？ Where have we met before?我们以前在哪里见过面？

This is true for sentences with be, sentences that have auxiliary verbs (e.g. They are waiting. She has finished.) and sentences with modal verbs (can, will, should, might, etc.).对于带有 be 的句子、带有助动词的句子（例如，他们正在等待。她已经完成了。）以及带有情态动词的句子（can、will、should、might 等）都是如此。

Questions in the present simple and past simple一般现在时和一般过去时的问题

For other verbs in the present simple, we use the auxiliary verb do/does in the question.对于一般现在时的其他动词，我们在问题中使用助动词 do/does。

Affirmative肯定 Question问题 Question with question word带有疑问词的问题
You work at home. 你在家工作。 Do you work at home?你在家工作吗？ Where do you work?你在哪里工作？
It costs £10. 售价 10 英镑。 Does it cost £10?花费 10 英镑吗？ How much does it cost?它要多少钱？

We use the auxiliary verb did in the past simple.我们使用助动词did的过去简单形式。

Affirmative肯定 Question问题 Question with question word带有疑问词的问题
She went home. 她回家了。 Did she go home?她回家了吗？ Where did she go?她去哪儿了？
They went to the cinema. 他们去了电影院。 Did they go to the cinema?他们去看电影了吗？ Where did they go?他们去哪儿了？

Subject questions主题问题

In some questions, who or what is the subject of the verb. There is no inversion of subject and verb in these questions.在某些问题中，谁或什么是动词的主语。这些问题中没有主语和动词的倒装。

Who broke the window? 谁打破了窗户？
Who is knocking on the door? 谁在敲门吗？
