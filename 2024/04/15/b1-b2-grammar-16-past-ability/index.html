【B1-B2-Grammar】16 Past ability-过去的能力

Look at these examples to see how could, was able to and managed to are used.查看这些示例，了解如何使用“could”、“was possible to”和“managed to”。

I could play the guitar when I was seven years old.我七岁时就会弹吉他。
The police weren’t able to catch the speeding car.警察没能抓住那辆超速行驶的汽车。

Grammar explanation-语法解释

General ability-一般能力

We usually use could or couldn’t to talk about general abilities in the past.过去我们通常用could或couldn’t来谈论一般能力。

She could paint before she started school.她在上学之前就会画画。
I couldn’t cook until I went to university.直到上了大学我才学会做饭。
When I lived next to the pool, I could go swimming every day.当我住在游泳池旁边时，我每天都可以去游泳。

Ability on one occasion – successful-一次能力——成功

When we talk about achieving something on a specific occasion in the past, we use was/were able to (= had the ability to) and managed to (= succeeded in doing something difficult).当我们谈论过去在特定场合取得某事时，我们使用 was/were able to (= 有能力) 和managed to (= 成功地做了一些困难的事情)。

The burglar was able to get in through the bathroom window.窃贼能够从浴室的窗户进入。
The burglar managed to get in through the bathroom window even though it was locked.尽管浴室的窗户是锁着的，窃贼还是设法从浴室的窗户钻了进去。

Could is not usually correct when we’re talking about ability at a specific moment in the past.当我们谈论过去某个特定时刻的能力时，“could”通常是不正确的。

Ability on one occasion – unsuccessful一次能力——不成功

When we talk about a specific occasion when someone didn’t have the ability to do something, we can use wasn’t/weren’t able to, didn’t manage to or couldn’t.当我们谈论某人没有能力做某事的特定场合时，我们可以使用 wast/weren’t able to、did’t manage to 或couldn’t。

The speaker wasn’t able to attend the conference due to illness.演讲者因病未能出席会议。
She couldn’t watch the match because she was working.因为她在工作，所以无法观看比赛。
They worked on it for months but they didn’t manage to find a solution.他们花了几个月的时间来研究这个问题，但没有找到解决方案。

Note that wasn’t/weren’t able to is more formal than couldn’t, while didn’t manage to emphasises that the thing was difficult to do.请注意，“wasn’t/weren’t able to”比“couldn’t”更正式，而“didn’t manage to”则强调这件事很难做到。
