Grammar explanation-语法解释

Used to + infinitive and be/get used to + -ing look similar but they have very different uses.used to + 不定式和 be/get used to + -ing 看起来很相似，但它们的用途却截然不同。

used to-习惯于

We use used to + infinitive to talk about a past situation that is no longer true. It tells us that there was a repeated action or state in the past which has now changed.我们用used to + 不定式来谈论过去不再真实的情况。它告诉我们过去重复的动作或状态现在已经改变了。

She used to be a long-distance runner when she was younger.她年轻时曾是一名长跑运动员。
I didn’t use to sleep very well, but then I started doing yoga and it really helps.我以前睡得不好，但后来我开始做瑜伽，它真的很有帮助。
Did you use to come here as a child?你小时候来过这里吗？

be used to and get used to-习惯并且习惯

Be used to means ‘be familiar with’ or ‘be accustomed to’.Beused to 的意思是“熟悉”或“习惯”。

She’s used to the city now and doesn’t get lost any more.她现在已经习惯了这座城市，不再迷路了。
He wasn’t used to walking so much and his legs hurt after the hike.他不习惯走那么多路，徒步之后他的腿就疼。
I’m a teacher so I’m used to speaking in public.我是一名老师，所以我习惯在公共场合演讲。

We use get used to to talk about the process of becoming familiar with something. 我们用 get used to 来谈论熟悉某事物的过程。

I’m finding this new job hard but I’m sure I’ll get used to it soon.我发现这份新工作很难，但我相信我很快就会适应它。
It took my mother years to get used to living in London after moving from Pakistan.我母亲从巴基斯坦搬到伦敦后花了很多年才适应伦敦的生活。
I’m getting used to the noise now. I found it really stressful when I first moved in.我现在已经习惯了噪音。当我刚搬进来的时候，我发现压力真的很大。

Be used to and get used to are followed by a noun, pronoun or the -ing form of a verb, and can be used about the past, present or future.be used to 和 get used to 后面接名词、代词或动词的 -ing 形式，可用于过去、现在或将来。
