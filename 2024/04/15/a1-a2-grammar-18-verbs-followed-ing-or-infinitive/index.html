Grammar explanation语法解释

A verb can be followed by another verb. The second one usually needs to change into the -ing form or the to + infinitive form. Which form you need depends on what the first verb is.一个动词后面可以跟另一个动词。第二个通常需要改为-ing形式或to+不定式形式。您需要哪种形式取决于第一个动词是什么。

Verbs followed by the -ing form动词后跟 -ing 形式

When enjoy, admit and mind are followed by another verb, it must be in the -ing form.当enjoy、accept和mind后面跟另一个动词时，它必须是-ing形式。

I enjoy travelling.我喜欢旅行。
He admitted stealing the necklace.他承认偷了项链。
I don’t mind waiting if you’re busy.如果你很忙的话我不介意等一下。

Other verbs in this group include avoid, can’t help, consider, dislike, feel like, finish, give up, miss, practise and suggest.该组中的其他动词包括避免、忍不住、考虑、不喜欢、感觉像、完成、放弃、错过、练习和建议。

Like and love can be followed by the -ing form and the to + infinitive form. They are both correct.Like 和 love 后面可以跟 -ing 形式和 to + 不定式形式。他们都是正确的。

Verbs followed by to + infinitive form动词后跟 to + 不定式形式

When want, learn and offer are followed by another verb, it must be in the to + infinitive form.当want、learn和offer后面跟另一个动词时，必须采用to+不定式形式。

I want to speak to the manager.我想和经理谈谈。
She’s learning to play the piano.她正在学习弹钢琴。
He offered to help us wash up.他主动提出帮我们洗漱。

Other verbs in this group include afford, agree, ask, choose, decide, expect, hope, plan, prepare, promise, refuse and would like.该组中的其他动词包括负担、同意、询问、选择、决定、期望、希望、计划、准备、承诺、拒绝和想要。
