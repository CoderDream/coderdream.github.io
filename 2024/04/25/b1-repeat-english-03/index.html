English-Chinese
Part One


    

  1. It doesn’t matter how well qualified you are, if your face doesn’t fit, you don’t stand a chance.
    不管你有多合格，如果你的脸不合适，你就没有机会。
    

    2. 

  2. In all our restaurants, smoking and non-smoking areas are segregated from each other.
    在我们所有的餐厅中，吸烟区和非吸烟区都是分开的。
    

    3. 

  3. I’m really sorry I’m late, Andrew. I forgot to set my alarm and I overslept.
    对不起，我来晚了，安德鲁。 我忘了设置闹钟，睡过头了。
    

    4. 

  4. We welcome you to join us on a special tour which explores this unique Australian attraction.
    我们欢迎您加入我们的特别之旅，探索这个独特的澳大利亚景点。
    

    5. 

  5. When we take many short vacation breaks, it gives us the opportunity to reboot our mental and emotional system.
    当我们有许多短暂的假期休息时，它给了我们重新启动心理和情绪系统的机会。
    

    6. 

  6. I just came back from a quick getaway and it was such a worthwhile experience.
    我刚从快速度假回来，这是一次非常值得的经历。
    

    7. 

  7. I came back from this trip totally satisfied, refreshed and ready for normal life again.
    我从这次旅行回来时完全满意，精神焕发，准备再次过正常生活。
    

    8. 

  8. I stayed at a very reasonably priced hotel in Montreal and my credit card got me a very nice discount.
    我住在蒙特利尔一家价格非常合理的酒店，我的信用卡给了我一个非常好的折扣。
    

    9. 

  9. The chain of hotel I stayed at provides free breakfasts in the morning, which helped me save on my meals.
    我住的连锁酒店早上提供免费早餐，这帮助我节省了餐费。
    

    10. 

  10. On this trip, I got to catch up with my New York friends whom I see maybe only once per year.
    在这次旅行中，我赶上了我的纽约朋友，我可能每年只见到他们一次。
    

    11. 

  11. There should be plenty of time to go shopping before we leave Sydney.
    在我们离开悉尼之前，应该有足够的时间去购物。
    

    12. 

  12. I went to New York last year, and I also spent some time in Washington.
    我去年去了纽约，在华盛顿也呆了一段时间。
    

    13. 

  13. I would like to discuss this project with you. if you are free sometime this week, let’s meet for lunch.
    我想和你讨论这个项目。如果你本周有空，让我们一起吃午饭。
    

    14. 

  14. Our company has its base in Vancouver, and branch offices all over the world.
    我们公司在温哥华设有总部，并在世界各地设有分支机构。
    

    15. 

  15. I locked myself out of our apartment and had to break in. I’m sorry. how careless of me.
    我把自己锁在公寓外面，不得不闯进去。 对不起。我多么粗心。
    

    16. 

  16. It’s no trouble at all; on the contrary, it will be a great pleasure to help you.
    一点也不麻烦;相反，很高兴能为您提供帮助。
    

    17. 

  17. What do you mean you can’t do it? It’ll be like shooting fish in a barrel.
    你什么意思你做不到？ 这就像在桶里射鱼一样。
    

    18. 

  18. This won’t do any good; on the contrary, it will make things even worse.
    这不会有任何好处;相反，它会让事情变得更糟。
    

    19. 

  19. It is not an idea around which the community can unite. on the contrary, I see it as one that will divide us.
    这不是一个社区可以团结起来的想法。相反，我认为它会分裂我们。
    

    20. 

  20. Mobile phones are inherently insecure, as anyone can listen to and record conversations.
    手机本质上是不安全的，因为任何人都可以收听和记录对话。
    

    21. 





Part Two


    

  1. Could we take a walk? I feel like a little exercise. it’s a relief to get out of the office once in a while.
    我们可以散散步吗？ 我感觉像是锻炼了一下。偶尔走出办公室是一种解脱。
    

    2. 

  2. Cooking up a quick dish doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice flavor. nor does fast food have to be junk food.
    做一道快餐并不意味着你必须牺牲味道。快餐也不一定是垃圾食品。
    

    3. 

  3. Don’t worry. you’ll get used to it soon. all artists have their own unique characteristics.
    不用担心。你很快就会习惯的。所有艺术家都有自己独特的特点。
    

    4. 

  4. All the artists have their own preferences and styles. without preference, there would be no art.
    所有的艺术家都有自己的喜好和风格。没有偏好，就没有艺术。
    

    5. 

  5. I dumped my boyfriend for another guy and I want him back. is it possible.
    我把我的男朋友甩给了另一个男人，我希望他回来。有可能吗？
    

    6. 

  6. My boyfriend left me for someone else after five years together and I took him back.
    我的男朋友在一起五年后离开了我，我把他带回来了。
    

    7. 

  7. What do you need your own computer for? You are welcome to use ours.
    你需要自己的电脑做什么？ 欢迎您使用我们的。
    

    8. 

  8. Don’t take it personally. you can’t stop people from saying what they think.
    不要把它当成个人。你无法阻止人们说出他们的想法。
    

    9. 

  9. Go away. don’t distract me. if I don’t get this finished in time, I’ll be in trouble.
    走开。不要分散我的注意力。如果我不及时完成这项工作，我会有麻烦的。
    

    10. 

  10. I don’t get lonely now because I make the effort to see people. why don’t you try talking to people.
    我现在不寂寞了，因为我努力去见人。你为什么不试着与人交谈。
    

    11. 

  11. That is all I know about them. I don’t really get a lot of information, and if I do I get it off mark.
    这就是我对他们的全部了解。 我真的没有得到很多信息，如果我得到，我就会得到它。
    

    12. 

  12. It’s not a pleasant feeling to discover you’ve been cheated by someone you trusted.
    发现自己被你信任的人欺骗了，这不是一种愉快的感觉。
    

    13. 

  13. Well, never mind, John, it’s not a matter of life and death. so let it go. there’s nothing you can do about it.
    好吧，没关系，约翰，这不是生死攸关的问题。所以放手吧。你对此无能为力。
    

    14. 

  14. An unsuitable bed is likely cause of back trouble. if the bed is soft, it will allow your body to sink into the bed.
    不合适的床可能是背部问题的原因。如果床是软的，它会让你的身体沉入床上。
    

    15. 

  15. If the mattress is too firm, it will push on your pressure points and cause back trouble.
    如果床垫太硬，它会压到您的压力点并导致背部问题。
    

    16. 

  16. Obviously, any child who receives dedicated teaching over an extended period is likely to improve.
    显然，任何长期接受专门教学的孩子都有可能进步。
    

    17. 

  17. Why don’t you settle your differences and be friends again? Tell me what happened between you and matt.
    你为什么不解决分歧，重新成为朋友呢？ 告诉我你和马特之间发生了什么。
    

    18. 

  18. It wasn’t easy at first, but now I have established a good working relationship with my boss.
    一开始并不容易，但现在我已经和老板建立了良好的工作关系。
    

    19. 

  19. From my point of view you are right。 I can’t agree with you more. you should be more confident in yourself.
    在我看来，你是对的。 我完全同意你的看法。你应该对自己更有信心。
    

    20. 

  20. I suppose your presence here today is not entirely coincidental. what do you want exactly.
    我想你今天来到这里并不完全是巧合。你到底想要什么。
    

    21. 





Part Three


    

  1. Save your time and energy to persuade me to change my mind. now get out of my face.
    节省你的时间和精力来说服我改变主意。现在离开我的脸。
    

    2. 

  2. There are two ways of payment. credit card or bank transfer. how would you like to pay.
    有两种付款方式。信用卡或银行转账。您希望如何付款。
    

    3. 

  3. Everybody has different music tastes, but I think we can all agree that we love Christmas music.
    每个人都有不同的音乐品味，但我想我们都同意我们喜欢圣诞音乐。
    

    4. 

  4. Now, you can watch your favorite hallmark channel Christmas movies and all new premieres 24 hours seven days a week.
    现在，您可以每周 7 天 24 小时观看您最喜爱的标志性频道圣诞电影和所有新的首映式。
    

    5. 

  5. Now that it is December, we’re counting down the days until Christmas. I can’t wait for that day to come.
    现在是 12 月，我们正在倒计时圣诞节的日子。 我迫不及待地想看到那一天的到来。
    

    6. 

  6. Christmas it’s our favorite time of the year, even though it involves spending all of our money on gifts and presents.
    圣诞节是我们一年中最喜欢的时间，尽管它涉及将我们所有的钱都花在礼物和礼物上。
    

    7. 

  7. Would you care for some tea, or even a light meal, to fortify yourself before your adventure.
    你会在冒险前喝点茶，甚至是一顿便餐来强化自己吗？
    

    8. 

  8. The woman who called yesterday wants to buy our house. what do you think.
    昨天打电话的女人想买我们的房子。你觉得怎么样。
    

    9. 

  9. Don’t give up hope. the situation may improve but if the worst comes to the worst I’ll have to sell the house.
    不要放弃希望。情况可能会有所改善，但如果最坏的情况发生，我将不得不卖掉房子。
    

    10. 

  10. If the worst comes to the worst and you lose your job, at least I will still be working.
    如果最坏的情况来了，你失去了工作，至少我还会工作。
    

    11. 

  11. I mean don’t get me wrong. Jenny is my best friend, but she can be kind of a pain sometimes.
    我的意思是不要误会我的意思。 珍妮是我最好的朋友，但她有时会有点痛苦。
    

    12. 

  12. One of my favorite things to do during every holiday. I get to decorate my dorm any way I want.
    每个假期我最喜欢做的事情之一。 我可以随心所欲地装饰我的宿舍。
    

    13. 

  13. I don’t know about you, but I love baking, especially during the holidays. baking is so much fun.
    我不了解你，但我喜欢烘焙，尤其是在假期。烘焙真是太有趣了。
    

    14. 

  14. You’ve made remarkable progress, considering you only started last week.
    考虑到你上周才开始，你已经取得了显着的进步。
    

    15. 

  15. He had been deceived by a young man claiming to be the son of a millionaire.
    他被一个自称是百万富翁之子的年轻人欺骗了。
    

    16. 

  16. We’ll have to be careful with money but that doesn’t mean we can’t enjoy ourselves.
    我们必须小心金钱，但这并不意味着我们不能享受自己。
    

    17. 

  17. This is a cheap and simple process however there are dangers we have to be careful not to cause any trouble.
    这是一个便宜而简单的过程，但是存在危险，我们必须小心不要造成任何麻烦。
    

    18. 

  18. The most likely outcome may be to subcontract much of the work to an outsider.
    最有可能的结果可能是将大部分工作分包给外部人员。
    

    19. 

  19. We arrived home early and surprised a burglar trying to break in.
    我们很早就到家了，一个试图闯入的窃贼感到惊讶。
    

    20. 

  20. It may take years to be completely accepted by the local community.
    可能需要数年时间才能被当地社区完全接受。
    

    21.
