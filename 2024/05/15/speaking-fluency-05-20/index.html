课程内容
通过本课，你将学习到 be on the point of 或be about to 的用法。
知识点
Can use the simple past form of ‘be’ + ‘about to’ (often with ‘just’) to talk about the immediate future from a point in the past.
Can use the simple past form of ‘be’ + ‘on the point of +’-ing’ to talk about things that were expected to happen soon after a point in the past.






在本节课中，你将看到 Linda 告诉Kana 自己的猫在昨晚走丢了的故事。请留意句中出现的be on the point of和be about to.

小试牛刀-Try it out!


I was about to go home when I received an email from my boss.


在前面的句子中，我们知道了说话者是在他被打扰的时候，即将要做某事了。


About to和on the point of都可以用来表示某件事即将发生。但是，about to 后面应该加上动词原形，而 on the point of 后面加动词 ing 形式。


因为这里的空格后面是动词原形，所以我们要选的是 about to。


I was about to go home when I received an email from my boss.
当我收到我老板的邮件的时候，我正要回家。


让我们再来试几道题吧！


They were on the point of giving up when they finally reached the top of the mountain.


John was about to take a shower when the doorbell rang.


I was on the point of going back home when my boss asked me to stay for an emergency meeting.


语法解释


我们可以用 be+about+to+动词原形表示我们预计某件事即将发生。
The airplane is about to take off.
飞机即将起飞了。


还可以用was/were+ about to 表达过去某一时间点之后即将发生的事。


I was about to write you a message when you called.
当你打电话来的时候我正打算给你写一条信息。


如果要强调某件事会在非常短的时间内发生，可以再加上just.


Just as + 主语 + was/were about to:
Just as we were about to leave, it started to rain.
就在我们要出发的时候，天下雨了。


主语 + was/were just about to:
Tom was just about to leave when his boss called him into her office.
当Tom正打算离开的时候，他的老板喊他去了她的办公室。


你也能用 was on the point of 来强调某件事非常接近发生。但是这个词组后接动词的话必须要加 ing。


接动词 ing:


I was on the point of quitting when my boss finally offered me a raise.
当我老板终于给我提加薪的时候，我都要辞职了。

It can be really tough when you feel like you’re in a dead-end job.
讲解
dead-end job 低薪且没有前途的工作。可以理解为”没有出路的工作”。dead end表示死路，死胡同。可以说come to/reach a dead end。在这个短语中dead-end做形容词，表示没有希望的。


We work to very tight schedules and don’t have much time to relax.
讲解
tight schedules 行程紧张。可以说某人work a tight schedule表示这个人的工作节奏很快。


I think you should take the initiative to make a change.
讲解
take the initiative 采取主动；首先采取行动。强调积极主动地做出行动，通常是为了改变目前的状况。
