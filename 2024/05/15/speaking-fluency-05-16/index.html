综合课-工作面试(上)-新课程

10分钟 57经验值
学习建议
Mindy最近在求职，她收到了一个面试邀请。
课程内容
通过本课，你可以学习到求职面试时的常用表达。

A: Hello, I’m Peter.

B: Hello, I’m Mindy. Nice to meet you.

A: Nice to meet you, too!

B: Thank you for rescheduling the interview. I had been doing a lot of volunteer work the night before the interview, so I was too tired the next day and wasn’t able to come to the interview.

A: Oh, no problem at all. Why don’t we start by having you tell me a little bit about yourself?

B: Sure. I just completed my master’s degree in architecture at Summer University. For the past three years, I have worked as an intern at a local architecture firm. I am also a volunteer at a local art school.

A: That’s very impressive. Why don’t you tell me more about why you want to work here in particular?

B: Well, this architecture firm has an excellent reputation. I like the company culture. Even though it is a small firm, it offers room for growth.

A: Great. Okay, now tell me more about your experience. What are your strengths?

B: I am organized and efficient. I can always finish projects ahead of schedule.

A: Excellent! One last question, why do you think we should choose you over other candidates?

B: Going to school while working part-time required a lot of discipline and time management skills. I can step into the next role and quickly be successful.

A: Okay, great! Thank you so much for coming in today, Mindy. We will be contacting the final candidates to come in for second interviews in two weeks.

B: Thank you! I appreciate this opportunity.

B: Thank you for rescheduling the interview. I had been doing a lot of volunteer work the night before the interview, so I was too tired the next day and wasn’t able to come to the interview.
B: 谢谢你重新安排面试。我前一天晚上做了很多志愿工作，所以第二天太累了，没能来参加面试。


A: Oh, no problem at all. Why don’t we start by having you tell me a little bit about yourself?
A: 哦，没问题。我们先从你介绍一下自己开始好吗？


B: Sure. I just completed my master’s degree in architecture at Summer University. For the past three years, I have worked as an intern at a local architecture firm. I am also a volunteer at a local art school.
B: 当然。我刚刚在夏季大学完成了我的硕士学位。在过去的三年里，我在一家本地的建筑公司担任实习生。我也是一家本地艺术学校的志愿者。


A: That’s very impressive. Why don’t you tell me more about why you want to work here in particular?
A: 这很令人印象深刻。你能多谈谈为什么你特别想在这里工作吗？


B: Well, this architecture firm has an excellent reputation. I like the company culture. Even though it is a small firm, it offers room for growth.
B: 嗯，这家建筑公司有着很好的声誉。我喜欢公司的文化。虽然它是一家小公司，但它提供了成长的空间。


A: Great. Okay, now tell me more about your experience. What are your strengths?
A: 很好。现在，告诉我更多关于你的经历。你的优点是什么？


B: I am organized and efficient. I can always finish projects ahead of schedule.
B: 我很有条理且高效。我总是能提前完成项目。


A: Excellent! One last question, why do you think we should choose you over other candidates?
A: 太好了！最后一个问题，为什么你认为我们应该选择你而不是其他候选人？


B: Going to school while working part-time required a lot of discipline and time management skills. I can step into the next role and quickly be successful.
B: 一边上学一边工作兼职要求了很多自律和时间管理技能。我可以迅速进入下一个角色并取得成功。


A: Okay, great! Thank you so much for coming in today, Mindy. We will be contacting the final candidates to come in for second interviews in two weeks.
A: 好的，很棒！非常感谢你今天来面试，明迪。我们将在两周内联系最终候选人来进行第二轮面试。


B: Thank you! I appreciate this opportunity.
B: 谢谢你！我感谢这个机会。

A volunteer does something to help others, without being paid for it. He works as a volunteer at a local hospital.


She was a volunteer in a kindergarten class last summer.


Will you be a volunteer in our animal rescue center?


He is a volunteer in a local middle school.


A reputation is the opinion that people have about somebody or something, based on what has happened in the past. Many students want to get into Harvard University because it has a great reputation.


She has the reputation of being a great film director.


Someone or something’s growth is their development and progress. The teacher is very pleased to see her students’ impressive growth this year.


A loving home is important for a child’s personal growth.


Our business is having rapid growth this year.


Experts say there will be a lot of job growth next year.


A strength is an advantage that can make someone successful. Knowing your own strengths will help you find a suitable job.


Knowing your own strengths will help you advance in your career.


The manager told him his strengths.


An organized person is able to plan things carefully and work efficiently. Mindy is very organized. She likes to keep a to-do list and she can always keep her desk tidy.


He is a very organized person. He always finishes his projects on time.


Ahead of schedule means earlier than planned. Due to the team’s hard work, the project was completed ahead of schedule.


We are ahead of schedule . The project is due in two weeks, but we only need two more days to finish our work.


We hope the project can be completed ahead of schedule.


If we can finish the job ahead of schedule,we may be able to take an early vacation.


We have interviewed some very strong candidates.


Discipline is the ability to control yourself. Keeping in good shape requires discipline.


For most people, living a healthy life requires a lot of discipline.


You need a lot of discipline to exercise every day.


Someone’s role is his or her position in an organization, society or relationship. As the president of the company, she plays an important role in the company’s success.


Social media is playing a major role in how we share information.


He has a lot of_ . He wakes up at 4 a.m. every day to start his work.
discipline


He evaluated one of the candidates to be very competitive for his strong self-discipline.


This job requires that candidates have strong communication skills.


A candidate is a person who is competing to get a job. There are ten candidates for the job.


Everyone has a role to play in protecting the environment.


An organized person is able to plan things carefully and work efficiently.
